Daily Reading Cash in Hand Update

Change:
- Daily Reading form now asks for Cash in Hand instead of manual Credit/Udhaar amount.
- Credit/Udhaar is auto calculated as: Gross Sale - Cash in Hand.
- Existing old data is not changed.
- New rows still store computed udhaar in transaction description so credit reconciliation with Transactions > New Sale continues to work.
- No new database table/column is required for this update.

Stock rule remains same:
- Daily Reading deducts only cash liters from stock.
- Customer credit liters are deducted when saved from Transactions > New Sale.
- This prevents double stock deduction.
