Daily Reading Combined Cash Update

- Daily Reading modal now has one Total Cash in Hand field for Petrol + Diesel combined cash.
- Per-machine/fuel cash input is removed from new reading flow.
- System calculates:
  Total Gross Sale = Petrol gross + Diesel gross
  Auto Credit = Total Gross Sale - Total Cash in Hand
- Daily Reading is now stock master for the entered machine liters in combined cash mode:
  Petrol/Diesel total machine liters are deducted from tanks when daily reading is saved.
- Transactions > New Sale credit entries are used to assign the auto credit to customer accounts.
- If the daily reading for that date is combined-cash mode, linked credit transactions do NOT deduct stock again.
- If no daily reading credit exists for that date, transaction warns as UNLINKED and deducts stock.
- Existing old data is not changed. No SQL is required.
